Important Notice:

This retirement calculator does not produce an official SBCERA estimate and SBCERA is not bound by the results.

This calculator shows your projected monthly lifetime retirement benefit based on the information entered and is calculated for the unmodified benefit option only. Your actual retirement benefit will be based on your date of retirement, highest final average compensation, verified service credit, and other factors that may differ from the factors used to calculate this estimate. Your benefit may also be subject to cost-of-living adjustments (COLAs), legislative and judicial legal actions, and your reciprocal rights, if any, from other pension systems. The calculator does not provide estimates for disability retirement benefits.

Reciprocal service credit only counts towards determining when you have accrued the minimum amount of service credit to become vested and eligible for retirement. It will not be included when calculating your retirement benefit. Therefore, it should not be included in your total years of service credit when using this calculator. However, any service credit purchased will be included when calculating your future retirement benefit and should be included for this calculator.

Please contact SBCERA if you have any questions or would like to request an official benefit estimate.

Definition of
Final Average Compensation


* Your final average compensation is one of the factors used to determine your lifetime benefit. For Tier 1 members, it is your highest 12-consecutive months of earnable compensation at any time during your membership or at another agency with which you have established reciprocity. For Tier 2 members, it is the average of your highest 36-consecutive months of pensionable compensation at any time during your membership with SBCERA or at another agency with which you have established reciprocity. For this calculator you must enter your average monthly final average compensation. Do not enter your annual compensation.

Service Credit under Different Retirement Formulas: Please use the Split Service Calculator if you have service credit under different retirement formulas (e.g. if you have service credit as a General and Safety member or as a Tier 1 and Tier 2 member.) The calculator above will not calculate benefit estimates for split plan service credit. Instead, you must use the split service calculator.

Final Average Compensation

Final Average Compensation – One of the factors used in determining your lifetime monthly retirement benefit. For Tier 1 members, it is your highest 12-consecutive months of earnable compensation at any time during your membership or another agency with which you have established reciprocity. For Tier 2 members, it is the average of your highest 36-consecutive months of pensionable compensation at any time during your membership with SBCERA.

Retirement Eligibility

Tier 1 Member (SBCERA membership date is prior to January 1, 2013)

  • Members Age 50 or older may retire with at least 10 years of service credit
  • Members Age 70 or older may retire at any time, with any amount of service credit
  • General members may retire, regardless of age, with at least 30 years of service credit
  • Safety members may retire, regardless of age, with at least 20 years of service credit
  • Active part-time members may retire at Age 55 or older with at least 10 years of membership and 5 years of service credit

Tier 2 Member (SBCERA membership date is on or after January 1, 2013)

  • General members at age 52 with at least 5 years of service credit.
  • Safety members at age 50 with at least 5 years of service credit.
  • Members Age 70 or older may retire at any time, with any amount of service credit
